## Company
TechVision Solutions

## Job Source
**Platform:** LinkedIn  
**Posted:** February 2026

## Job Title
Junior Software Developer (Full-time)

## About the Role
TechVision Solutions is seeking a Junior Software Developer to join our growing development team. You'll work on web applications, APIs, and cloud-based solutions for our enterprise clients. This is a great opportunity to develop your career in a collaborative environment with mentorship from senior developers.

## Key Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain web applications using modern JavaScript frameworks (React, Vue, or similar)
- Write clean, maintainable code and participate in code reviews
- Work with backend developers to integrate APIs and services
- Assist in debugging and resolving application issues
- Contribute to technical documentation and knowledge sharing
- Participate in agile development cycles (sprints, standups, retrospectives)
- Collaborate with UI/UX designers to implement design specifications

## Required Skills
- Proficiency in JavaScript/TypeScript
- Experience with HTML5, CSS3, and responsive design
- Familiarity with version control (Git)
- Understanding of REST APIs and HTTP protocols
- Knowledge of at least one front-end framework (React, Vue, Angular)
- Strong problem-solving and analytical skills
- Excellent communication and teamwork abilities
- Basic SQL and database concepts

## Experience & Qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field (or equivalent work experience)
- 1-2 years of professional development experience or solid portfolio of projects
- Familiarity with DevOps or CI/CD pipelines is a plus
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP) is advantageous
- Knowledge of testing frameworks and automated testing is beneficial

## Salary & Benefits
- $55,000 - $65,000 per annum (based on experience)
- Flexible work arrangements (2 days remote, 3 days on-site)
- Professional development budget
- Health and wellness programs
- 20 days annual leave + public holidays
